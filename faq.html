Ordering

How do I start a custom project? +

Start by using the project request form on the homepage. Share what you want made, how it will be used, any size or quantity details, and whether you have a file, photo, sketch, or example.

You can start here: Start Your Project.

Do I need a 3D file already? +

No. A 3D file helps, but it is not required. You can send a photo, drawing, rough idea, broken part, measurement, inspiration image, or description and we’ll help determine whether it can be designed or printed.

Can OliPoly help design something from an idea? +

Yes, depending on the project. Some ideas may need design time, measurements, test prints, or revisions before the final piece is ready. Design work may affect the quote.

Can I upload reference files or photos? +

Yes. After submitting a project request, you can upload sketches, photos, STL/3MF files, screenshots, or reference images using the upload form linked on the homepage.

Upload here: Open Upload Form.

Pricing & Quotes

How are prices calculated? +

Pricing depends on material, print time, size, complexity, design work, quantity, finishing, packaging, shipping, and any special requirements. Custom projects are usually quoted before production begins.

Do custom orders require a deposit? +

Some custom orders may require a deposit before production begins. The quote will explain whether a deposit is required and when the remaining balance is due.

Can I request changes after receiving a quote? +

Yes. If you need changes, reply before approving the quote. Changes after approval may affect pricing, timing, or feasibility, especially if production has already started.

What happens after I accept a quote? +

Once accepted, your quote can become an order number beginning with OP-. You’ll be able to track progress through the order tracker and complete payment using the available payment options.

Track here: Track Order.

Production & Materials

How long does a custom print take? +

Timing depends on design complexity, print time, material availability, current order volume, and whether revisions or test prints are needed. Typical timing will be included in your quote when possible.

What materials do you offer? +

Common materials may include PLA, PETG, and other specialty filaments depending on the project. If you are not sure what material you need, OliPoly can recommend an option based on how the item will be used.

Are 3D printed items perfectly smooth? +

Not usually. 3D printed items may show layer lines, small texture differences, seams, or slight variation in color and finish. That is normal for printed parts and handmade/custom production.

Can you color-match prints? +

Exact color matching is not guaranteed, but we can usually help choose a close material color or suggest options that fit the project.

Pickup, Shipping & Tracking

Do you offer local pickup? +

Yes, local pickup may be available depending on the order and arrangement. Pickup details will be confirmed before completion or delivery.

Do you ship orders? +

Shipping may be available depending on item size, durability, packaging needs, and destination. Shipping costs and timing can be included with your quote or final order details.

How does order tracking work? +

Accepted orders may receive an OP- order number. You can enter that number on the tracker page to view customer-safe order status updates, payment status, next steps, and pickup/shipping notes.

Go to: Track Order.

Business, Prototypes & Replacement Parts

Can you make replacement parts? +

Often, yes. Replacement parts depend on size, function, material needs, measurements, and whether the part can be reasonably recreated. Some parts may require test fitting or revisions.

Can you help with prototypes? +

Yes. OliPoly can help with concept models, fit checks, small prototype parts, mockups, and early design iterations. Customers are responsible for validating final suitability for technical, commercial, safety, or production use.

Can you handle small production runs? +

Small batch or repeat production may be available depending on the part, quantity, timeline, material, and printer capacity. Bulk pricing may be quoted separately.

Can businesses request quotes or purchase-order support? +

Yes. For business projects, OliPoly can provide formal quotes and may support vendor paperwork, W-9 requests, and purchase-order style workflows when appropriate.

General Questions

Are 3D printed items food-safe? +

Unless specifically stated in writing, OliPoly 3D printed items should not be assumed to be food-safe. Layer lines, material type, coating, cleaning method, and use conditions all matter.

Are prints safe for heavy loads or structural use? +

Unless specifically agreed in writing, prints are not guaranteed for structural, load-bearing, safety-critical, or high-stress applications. Customers are responsible for evaluating suitability for the intended use.

Can you print copyrighted characters, logos, or designs? +

Customers are responsible for ensuring they have the right to use any submitted designs, logos, artwork, models, or requested content. OliPoly 3D may decline projects that appear to raise ownership or intellectual property concerns.

You can review the full terms, privacy policy, refund language, payment details, and custom-order notes on the legal page.
