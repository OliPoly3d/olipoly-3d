Featured concept 02

Sports-spirit pieces can start small.

Keychains • bag tags • small gifts

Small pieces are often the smartest first test.

Keychains, bag tags, locker-style pieces, ornaments, coach gifts, senior-night items, and small batch keepsakes are easier to quote, test, and refine before moving into larger signs or display pieces.

  • Good for names, numbers, dates, and team colors
  • Can be single-color, two-color, or clean multicolor
  • Works well for small local batches or one-off gifts
For school, team, or organization marks, protected logos and mascot artwork should only be used when the customer has permission or provides approved artwork. Original spirit-inspired designs are also an option.
Featured concept 03

A flat idea can become a character or yard piece.

Character & decor concepts

From 2D artwork to a real object.

A flat image, mascot idea, drawing, or theme can sometimes be reimagined as a 3D character, shelf piece, porch decoration, or yard-display concept. Larger pieces usually need simplification, print planning, assembly, and finish work.

  • Small display pieces may print as one piece
  • Pieces over about 10 inches may need to be printed in sections
  • Outdoor use may require material and finish choices that fit the environment
This type of project is best treated as a custom concept conversation. The final design can be made more original, simplified for printing, sized for the intended use, or adapted around approved art.

Aurora 3D printing FAQ

Questions local customers usually ask.

Can you make something from only a photo or sketch?

Often, yes. A photo or sketch is usually enough to start the conversation. Some ideas need design work before they are printable.

Do you make one item, or only batches?

Both. Single gifts, one replacement-style part, and small custom batches are all good fits.

Can you make Aurora sports or school-spirit items?

Yes, for original ideas, customer-provided artwork, names, colors, and approved designs. Protected marks, school logos, or organization artwork should only be used when permission exists.

Can larger yard decorations be printed?

Sometimes. Larger pieces usually need to be split into sections, printed in multiple parts, assembled, and possibly painted or sealed depending on the final use.

Have an Aurora idea worth printing?

Start with whatever you have: a photo, old memory, sketch, screenshot, broken part, measurements, or a rough idea. OliPoly can help turn it into a quote-ready project.
