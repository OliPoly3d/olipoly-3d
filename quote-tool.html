Use this page to estimate pricing before you make the order. It separates direct costs like filament, packaging, shipping, and hardware from overhead like machine wear, design time, marketplace fees, and profit margin.

How to think about it

Direct costs are tied to the order. Overhead covers the business. Margin is what you want left after both.

A good starting workflow: pick a preset, enter material, packaging, shipping, any hardware, design/print time, and your target margin. Then review the customer quote view before sending.
