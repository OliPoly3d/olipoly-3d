Ordering

How do I start a custom project?+

Use the Start Project page. Share what you want made, how it will be used, size or quantity details, and any file, photo, sketch, drawing, or example that helps explain it.

You can start here: Start Your Project.

Do I need a 3D file already?+

No. A 3D file helps, but you can also send a photo, drawing, broken part, measurement, inspiration image, or plain description. If you provide STL, 3MF, CAD, drawing, or reference files, those files are treated as customer-supplied specifications unless separate design review is requested.

Can OliPoly help design something from an idea?+

Yes, depending on the project. Some ideas need design time, measurements, test prints, or revisions. Any design work will be reflected in the quote. Final fit, use, and performance should still be confirmed by the customer.

Can I upload reference files or photos?+

Yes. The Start Project page accepts helpful project details and reference uploads, including photos, sketches, STL/3MF files, screenshots, drawings, and PDFs.

Start here: Start Your Project.

What happens after I approve a quote?+

Once a quote is approved and any required deposit, payment, PO, or customer documentation is received, the project can move into scheduling and production. Accepted quotes may convert from a Q- quote number into an OP- order number for tracking.

Pricing & Quotes

How are prices calculated?+

Pricing can include material, print time, machine time, design work, post-processing, packaging, shipping, quantity, complexity, and timeline. Business or repeat orders may include quantity pricing, part references, tax-exempt handling, or PO terms.

How long is a quote valid?+

Unless otherwise stated, quotes are generally valid for 30 days. Material availability, scope changes, quantity changes, rush timing, or new requirements may require an updated quote.

Can the price change after approval?+

The approved price is based on the scope, quantity, files, materials, finish, and assumptions listed at the time of quoting. Changes after approval may affect price, timing, or feasibility.

Do custom orders require a deposit?+

Some custom orders require a deposit before production begins, especially design-heavy, large, bulk, material-heavy, or business orders. Any deposit or payment requirement will be shown on the quote or invoice.

Production & Materials

What materials do you print with?+

Material options vary by project and availability. Common options may include PLA, PETG, specialty colors, and other materials when appropriate. Material choice depends on appearance, durability, temperature exposure, flexibility, and intended use.

Will 3D printed parts look perfectly smooth?+

3D printed parts are layer-manufactured, so visible layer lines, small surface variation, texture differences, support marks, seam locations, and slight color/finish variation are normal unless special finishing is quoted separately.

How accurate are the parts?+

Accuracy depends on geometry, material, printer settings, orientation, scale, wall thickness, and the file provided. If a tight fit or critical dimension matters, tell us before quoting so test fitting, tolerance discussion, or revisions can be planned.

Are customer-supplied files checked for issues?+

Basic printability issues may be noticed during setup, but customer-supplied files are generally manufactured as provided. OliPoly 3D is not responsible for design flaws, incorrect scale, fitment issues, functional failure, or bad geometry from customer-provided files unless separate design review or redesign work is agreed to.

Can you guarantee a part will work for my exact use?+

Unless specifically agreed in writing, the customer is responsible for confirming fit, function, installation, safety, and suitability for the intended use. Prototype and replacement parts may require testing or revisions.

Pickup, Delivery & Shipping

Do you offer local pickup?+

Local pickup may be available by arrangement. Pickup details are confirmed before completion or handoff.

Do you ship orders?+

Shipping may be available depending on size, durability, packaging needs, and destination. Costs and timing can be included with the quote or order details. Carrier delays, weather disruptions, and carrier handling are outside of OliPoly 3D’s control once shipped.

How does order tracking work?+

Accepted orders may receive an OP- order number. Enter it on the tracker to view customer-safe status, next steps, payment status, and pickup/shipping notes.

Go to: Track Order.

Business, PO Work & Production

Can you make replacement parts?+

Often, yes. Fit, function, material, measurements, and testing needs all matter. Some replacement parts may require revisions, test fitting, or customer validation before regular use.

Can you help with prototypes?+

Yes. OliPoly can help with concept models, fit checks, mockups, prototype parts, and early design iterations. Final suitability should be validated by the customer for the intended use.

Can you handle small production runs?+

Yes, when the part and timeline are a good fit. Repeat runs, small batches, part numbers, revision tracking, packaging requirements, and quantity pricing can be discussed during quoting.

Do you support purchase orders and business invoices?+

Yes. Business projects can use formal quotes, PO numbers, invoices, agreed payment terms, W-9 requests, tax-exempt documentation, and vendor setup support when appropriate.

Do you support tax-exempt customers?+

Yes. A completed tax exemption certificate should be provided before tax is removed from a qualifying business order. OliPoly keeps the certificate on file with the customer record when applicable.

Can businesses reorder parts later?+

Yes. If files, specs, and production notes are saved from the original order, repeat orders can usually be handled more efficiently. Include the prior quote, order, invoice, PO number, part number, or revision when reaching out.

Are parts certified for structural or safety-critical use?+

Unless specifically stated in writing, OliPoly 3D parts are not certified for life-safety, medical, aerospace, automotive safety, pressure, high-heat, or structural load-bearing applications. Customers are responsible for validation and testing for the intended use.

General Questions

Are 3D printed items food-safe?+

Unless specifically stated in writing, OliPoly 3D printed items should not be assumed to be food-safe. Material, coating, cleaning, layer lines, and use conditions all matter.

Are prints safe for heavy loads or structural use?+

Unless specifically agreed in writing, prints are not guaranteed for structural, load-bearing, safety-critical, or high-stress use. Customers are responsible for evaluating suitability.

Can you print copyrighted characters, logos, or designs?+

Customers are responsible for having the right to use submitted designs, logos, artwork, models, or requested content. OliPoly may decline projects with ownership or IP concerns.

Where can I read the full terms and policies?+

You can review the full terms, privacy policy, refund language, payment details, and custom-order notes on the Legal page.
