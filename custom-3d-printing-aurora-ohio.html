Featured local concepts

From flat logo or photo to physical object.

Keychains & small gifts

Small pieces are often the best starting point.

Keychains, magnets, tags, and ornaments are usually easier to quote, easier to test, and easier to make in small batches. They are a good way to try a local design before moving into larger decor or display pieces.

  • Great for team gifts, favors, and keepsakes
  • Can be simple single-color or clean multicolor
  • Good fit for local nostalgia and event ideas
Character & yard decor

Logo-to-character concepts can become decor.

A flat image can sometimes be reimagined as a 3D character, mascot-style shelf piece, or yard decoration. Larger pieces may need to be simplified, printed in sections, assembled, and painted or finished by hand.

  • Small display pieces can often print as one piece
  • Larger decor may be seamed together from multiple parts
  • Color, finish, outdoor use, and size affect price

Aurora 3D printing FAQ

Questions local customers usually ask.

Can you make something from only a photo or sketch?

Often, yes. A photo or sketch is enough to start the conversation. Some ideas need design work before they are printable.

Do you make one item, or only batches?

Both. Single gifts, one replacement-style part, and small custom batches are all good fits.

Can you make Aurora sports or school spirit items?

Yes, for customer-provided artwork, names, colors, and approved designs. Protected marks, school logos, or organization artwork should only be used when the customer has permission.

Can larger yard decorations be printed?

Sometimes. Larger pieces usually need to be split into sections, printed in multiple parts, assembled, and possibly painted or sealed depending on the final use.

Have an Aurora idea worth printing?

Start with whatever you have: a photo, old logo, sketch, screenshot, broken part, measurements, or a rough idea. OliPoly can help turn it into a quote-ready project.
