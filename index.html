Meet Poly 🐝

Poly was dreamed up by Oli — Olive, age 8 — from OliPoly 3D.

After seeing how busy we were bringing custom ideas to life, Olive said we were basically busy as a bee printing — and Poly was born.

Now Poly helps welcome people to the site, guide new ideas, and remind everyone that custom 3D printing can be creative, friendly, and fun.

Careful though… once you see your idea turn into a real print, the printing bug may bite you too!
