Most OliPoly 3D products are custom-made. Because of that, final printed parts may show normal variation in color, texture, layer lines, and finish depending on the material, print settings, and design.

Unless specifically stated in writing, printed parts are not designed, tested, or guaranteed for structural, load-bearing, safety-critical, or high-stress applications. Customers are responsible for evaluating whether a design is suitable for its intended use.

Quotes and Approval

Quotes are provided for review before production. A quote number may be issued in the format Q-######. Once approved, it may be converted into an order number in the format OP-###### for production and tracking purposes.

Production may begin only after quote approval and any required deposit or payment has been received.

Payments

Payments may be accepted through third-party providers such as Stripe, PayPal, and Venmo. In some cases, cash may also be accepted for pickup or approved local delivery.

Payment is typically required before pickup, delivery, or shipping unless otherwise agreed. Custom orders may require a deposit before production begins, with any remaining balance due at completion.

If an order is not paid for or picked up within a reasonable timeframe after completion, OliPoly 3D reserves the right to cancel, resell, or dispose of the item at its discretion.

Revisions and Changes

Changes requested after approval may affect pricing, timeline, or feasibility. Additional charges may apply when revisions require new design work, re-slicing, re-printing, or material changes.

Pickup, Delivery, and Shipping

Orders may be provided by pickup, approved local delivery, or shipping, depending on the project and arrangement. Customers are responsible for confirming shipping addresses and pickup or delivery details.

Customers are responsible for providing accurate contact, shipping, and order information. OliPoly 3D is not responsible for issues resulting from incorrect or incomplete information provided by the customer.

Returns and Refunds

Refund decisions, if any, are handled on a case-by-case basis. Refunds are not guaranteed and are issued solely at the discretion of OliPoly 3D.
