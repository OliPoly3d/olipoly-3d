Custom Work

Most OliPoly 3D products are custom-made or small-batch manufactured. Because of that, final printed parts may show normal variation in color, texture, layer lines, seam location, support marks, and finish depending on material, geometry, print orientation, and production settings.

Unless specifically stated in writing, printed parts are not designed, tested, certified, or guaranteed for structural, load-bearing, life-safety, medical, aerospace, automotive safety, pressure, high-heat, or other safety-critical applications. Customers are responsible for evaluating whether a design is suitable for its intended use.

OliPoly 3D may work on concept models, decorative items, business orders, low-volume production runs, replacement-style parts, prototypes, and fit-check parts. Customers are responsible for validating suitability for commercial, technical, production, installation, and end-use purposes.

Quotes and Approval

Quotes are provided for review before production. A quote number may be issued in the format Q-######. Once approved, it may be converted into an order number in the format OP-###### for production and tracking purposes.

Unless otherwise stated, quotes are generally valid for 30 days. Pricing is based on the specifications, quantities, files, materials, finish, delivery expectations, and assumptions available at the time of quoting.

Production may begin only after quote approval and any required deposit, payment, purchase order, tax-exempt documentation, vendor setup, or customer confirmation has been received.

Commercial Terms

  • Lead times are estimates unless specifically agreed in writing.
  • Lead times begin after approval and required payment/documentation are complete.
  • Customer-supplied files and specifications are manufactured as provided unless design review is separately requested.
  • Changes after approval may require updated pricing, lead time, or production review.
  • Custom-manufactured items are generally non-returnable once production has started.
  • Customer is responsible for verifying fit, function, installation, intended use, and any required testing.

Payments

Payments may be accepted through third-party providers such as Stripe, PayPal, and Venmo. In some cases, cash may also be accepted for pickup or approved local delivery.

Payment is typically required before pickup, delivery, or shipping unless otherwise agreed. Custom orders may require a deposit before production begins, with any remaining balance due at completion. Business orders may follow approved PO, invoice, or customer-standard terms when agreed in writing.

Unclaimed Orders

If an order is not paid for or picked up within a reasonable timeframe after completion, OliPoly 3D reserves the right to cancel, resell, recycle, or dispose of the item at its discretion.

Production and Timing

Estimated lead times are provided in good faith but are not guaranteed. Timing may vary based on order complexity, material availability, machine maintenance, design revisions, volume, test prints, packaging needs, and other business or production factors.

Because many orders are custom, approved custom work is generally not returnable once production has started. If there is an issue with an order, customers should contact OliPoly 3D promptly so a reasonable resolution can be reviewed.
