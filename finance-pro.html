Track pre-tax sales, tax collected, real costs, shipping, mileage, and monthly profit.

Income entries use the sale amount before tax, then calculate sales tax from the rate you enter. Expense entries can capture vendor details, business-use percentages, receipt links, mileage-based deductions for delivery and supply runs, plus home office helper defaults.

Best use for your workflow For income, enter the taxable sale amount before tax, choose the destination county, and enter the tax rate. For mileage, switch to an expense entry and choose Vehicle / Delivery Mileage to auto-calculate the deductible amount from miles and rate.
