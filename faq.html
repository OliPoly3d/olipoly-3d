Ordering

How do I start a custom project? +

Use the Start Project page. Share what you want made, how it will be used, size or quantity details, and any file, photo, sketch, drawing, or example that helps explain it.

You can start here: Start Your Project.

Do I need a 3D file already? +

No. A 3D file helps, but you can also send a photo, drawing, broken part, measurement, inspiration image, or plain description. We’ll help determine what is realistic.

Can OliPoly help design something from an idea? +

Yes, depending on the project. Some ideas need design time, measurements, test prints, or revisions. Any design work will be reflected in the quote.

Can I upload reference files or photos? +

Yes. The Start Project page accepts helpful project details and reference uploads, including photos, sketches, STL/3MF files, screenshots, drawings, and PDFs.

Start here: Start Your Project.

Pricing & Quotes

How are prices calculated? +

Pricing depends on material, print time, size, complexity, design work, quantity, finishing, packaging, shipping, and special requirements. Custom work is quoted before production begins.

Do custom orders require a deposit? +

Some custom orders require a deposit before production. Your quote will clearly state the deposit, balance, and payment timing.

Can I request changes after receiving a quote? +

Yes. If you need changes, reply before approving the quote. Changes after approval may affect pricing, timing, or feasibility, especially if production has already started.

What happens after I accept a quote? +

Once accepted, your quote can become an OP- order number. You can then track progress, payment status, and customer-safe updates through the tracker.

Track here: Track Order.

Production & Materials

How long does a custom print take? +

Timing depends on design complexity, print time, materials, order volume, and whether test prints or revisions are needed. Expected timing is included when possible.

What materials do you offer? +

Common materials may include PLA, PETG, and specialty filaments depending on the project. If you are unsure, OliPoly can recommend a material based on use.

Are 3D printed items perfectly smooth? +

Not usually. Layer lines, seams, texture, and small color or finish variations are normal for custom 3D printed parts.

Can you color-match prints? +

Exact color matching is not guaranteed, but we can usually help choose a close material color or suggest options that fit the project.

Pickup, Shipping & Tracking

Do you offer local pickup? +

Yes. Local pickup may be available by arrangement. Pickup details are confirmed before completion or handoff.

Do you ship orders? +

Shipping may be available depending on size, durability, packaging needs, and destination. Costs and timing can be included with the quote or order details.

How does order tracking work? +

Accepted orders may receive an OP- order number. Enter it on the tracker to view customer-safe status, next steps, payment status, and pickup/shipping notes.

Business, PO Work & Production

Can you make replacement parts? +

Often, yes. Fit, function, material, measurements, and testing needs all matter. Some replacement parts may require revisions or test fitting.

Can you help with prototypes? +

Yes. OliPoly can help with concept models, fit checks, mockups, prototype parts, and early design iterations. Final suitability should be validated by the customer for the intended use.

Can you handle small production runs? +

Yes, when the part and timeline are a good fit. Repeat runs, small batches, part numbers, and quantity pricing can be discussed during quoting.

Do you support purchase orders and business invoices? +

Yes. Business projects can use formal quotes, PO numbers, invoices, agreed payment terms, W-9 requests, and vendor setup support when appropriate.

Do you support tax-exempt customers? +

Yes. A completed tax exemption certificate should be provided before tax is removed from a qualifying business order. OliPoly keeps the certificate on file with the customer record.

Can businesses reorder parts later? +

Yes. If files, specs, and production notes are saved from the original order, repeat orders can usually be handled more efficiently. Include the prior quote, order, invoice, or PO number when reaching out.

Do you accept purchase orders? +

Yes. Business customers can provide a PO number, and OliPoly can reference that PO on the order, tracker, invoice, and related documents.

Can you support tax exempt customers? +

Yes, when a completed exemption certificate is provided and kept on file. Tax exempt business orders can be marked accordingly so sales tax is not charged on the quote/invoice.

Can businesses reorder the same part later? +

Yes. For repeatable business parts, it helps to keep the PO number, part number, revision, material, color, and approved production files together so future reorders are easier and more consistent.

General Questions

Are 3D printed items food-safe? +

Unless specifically stated in writing, OliPoly 3D printed items should not be assumed to be food-safe. Material, coating, cleaning, layer lines, and use conditions all matter.

Are prints safe for heavy loads or structural use? +

Unless specifically agreed in writing, prints are not guaranteed for structural, load-bearing, safety-critical, or high-stress use. Customers are responsible for evaluating suitability.

Can you print copyrighted characters, logos, or designs? +

Customers are responsible for having the right to use submitted designs, logos, artwork, models, or requested content. OliPoly may decline projects with ownership or IP concerns.

You can review the full terms, privacy policy, refund language, payment details, and custom-order notes on the legal page.
