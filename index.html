Frequently asked questions

Do I need a design file?

No. A simple description is enough to get started. You can send sketches, references, or files later if you have them.

What materials do you offer?

Common materials include PLA, PETG, and ABS. If you're not sure what you need, we’ll help you choose the best option.

How long does it take?

Most projects are reviewed within 24 hours. Production time depends on size and complexity, but we’ll outline timing up front.

Can you help design my idea?

Yes. If you don’t have a finished design, we can help refine your idea and guide you toward something that prints well.

What types of projects do you take?

Everything from gifts and custom pieces to functional parts and prototypes. If you have an idea, it’s worth asking.

How do I get started?

Just fill out the form below with a short description. We’ll follow up with next steps and options within 24 hours.
