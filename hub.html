Workflow Playbooks

Customer-type operating flows

Use these playbooks as the current operating guide for retail, business/PO, craft-show, and repeat/catalog work.

Retail / custom customerStandard quote approval, payment, production, pickup or shipping.+
1Receive inquiry or custom request.
2Create quote in Quote Tool.
3Generate Quote PDF v2 and prepare Quote Email v2.
4Send approval link to customer.
5Customer approves quote on Quote Response page.
6Quote converts to OP order and activates Tracker.
7Confirm payment link/status in Orders Admin.
8Produce item; generate traveler/packing slip if helpful.
9Send ready, pickup, shipped, or completion lifecycle email.
10Record sale/tax/costs in Finance Pro.
Best for one-off custom jobs, gifts, small creative builds, local pickup, and normal customer requests.
Business / PO customerProfessional quote, vendor docs, PO, live order docs, invoice.+
1Receive RFQ, drawings, part details, or print files.
2Create professional/business quote with PO, part, and revision fields.
3Send Quote PDF v2 + Quote Email v2; include W-9/vendor packet if requested.
4Customer approves quote or issues PO.
5Convert quote to OP order and confirm PO/part/revision data.
6Generate Manufacturing Traveler for production.
7Generate Packing Slip from Orders Admin for fulfillment.
8Ship/deliver and send shipping or ready-for-pickup email.
9Generate/send Invoice PDF v2 or invoice email as required.
10Close out in Finance Pro and save catalog part if repeatable.
Best for PO customers, repeat production, part-numbered jobs, business invoices, and purchasing department workflows.
Craft show / in-person saleFast payment, handoff, optional follow-up, Finance Pro entry.+
1Customer selects premade item or asks about a custom idea.
2Open Pay Page or QR/payment sign.
3Accept card, PayPal, Venmo, or other approved payment.
4Bag item with thank-you insert or QR card.
5For custom follow-up, direct customer to quote form/site.
6Record sale, tax collected, and costs in Finance Pro.
7Add reminder if follow-up/custom quote is needed.
Best for events, quick local purchases, impulse sales, and converting in-person ideas into later quote requests.
Repeat / catalog productionSaved part memory, revision control, repeat job setup.+
1Open Orders Admin and search saved catalog parts.
2Load the catalog part into the order form for reference.
3Confirm revision, material, color, quantity, and prior pricing.
4Create quote or order using current quantity/pricing.
5Generate traveler and packing slip from live order data.
6Produce batch and update Tracker.
7Send lifecycle email and close out in Finance Pro.
8Update catalog part notes/revision if anything changed.
The Create Reorder Quote automation is intentionally on the backlog; for now, use catalog search/load as the memory layer.
